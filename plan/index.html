Getting Here

From the airport to your door

1

A quick 10-minute drive from MYR

Moore Memories is about 6 miles (~10 minutes) from Myrtle Beach International Airport. Head south on US-17 Bypass / S Kings Hwy; Ocean Lakes sits on S Kings Hwy at Highway 544. From farther out, US-501 and Hwy 31 feed into the area, then take Hwy 544 east toward the ocean.

2

GPS the park first, then the site

Set navigation to the main entrance — 6001 S Kings Hwy, Myrtle Beach, SC 29575 — to reach the manned gate every time. Don’t rely on the street address (1669 Starfish Rd) alone, as in-park roads may not route correctly. Inside, follow signs to Starfish Road and Site 1669.

3

The main gate at Hwy 544 (manned 24/7)

Ocean Lakes is private and gated. The main entrance is staffed by Security around the clock, 365 days a year, and every vehicle is checked for a valid car pass before entering.

4

Car pass for every vehicle

Each vehicle must display a car pass on the mirror. Two passes come with your stay; extra passes carry a daily fee. Get them by registering in person at the Main Office, or via Express Check-In.

5

Recommended: Express Check-In

Request it 2+ weeks ahead and Ocean Lakes mails your car passes before you arrive — so you skip the office, show your pass at the gate, and drive straight to Site 1669.

6

Arriving at the home

Pull into the new concrete driveway (parking for 3). You’ll know it by the covered metal-roof porch, composite deck, and turf yard. Everything’s ready and waiting — just unload and settle in.

7

Getting around

Ocean Lakes spans 310 golf-cart-friendly acres. From Site 1669 the beach, pools, and Sandy Mart are a short, easy ride. Keep your car pass displayed for the whole stay.

Gated & secure — Ocean Lakes is a fully gated resort with Security at the Highway 544 main gate 24/7, year-round. Every vehicle is checked for a valid car pass before entry, and patrols monitor the grounds — a big part of why families feel safe here.

House Rules & Policies

The fine print, made friendly

Check-in & check-out

Check-in 4:00 PM, check-out 10:00 AM. Peak summer is Saturday-to-Saturday; shorter stays are often available off-season. Please respect check-out so we can prepare the home for the next guests.

Sleeps up to 6

A private rear queen bedroom plus a loft with two queen beds. Maximum occupancy of 6 is enforced and the party size must be disclosed at booking.

Primary renter 25+

The primary renter must be at least 25 and present for the whole stay. No third-party or "chaperone" bookings. Photo ID may be required at check-in.

Family-friendly only

A family vacation home in a family campground — no house parties, events, or non-family groups under 25. Ocean Lakes is gated with on-site security.

Quiet hours 11 PM – 7 AM

Per Ocean Lakes policy, keep noise respectful overnight so neighboring families can rest.

No smoking or vaping

100% smoke-free indoors and on the porch/deck. Evidence of smoking forfeits the deposit and incurs cleaning charges.

Pets — ask first

Please request and get written approval before booking. Ocean Lakes’ standard policy is no pets except certified service animals, which are always welcome.

Parking & gate passes

Driveway fits up to 3 vehicles. Ocean Lakes requires a paid gate pass for every vehicle (~$12–$15/day, paid to the campground). Two passes typically come with the reservation.

Using a golf cart

Carts aren’t included, but many guests bring or rent one. If you do, the driver must be 21+ with a license, observe Ocean Lakes’ cart curfew, and never drive after drinking or on the beach.

Care of the home

Treat it as your own: tidy up, bag trash, keep doors closed while the A/C runs, no candles/open flames. Guests are responsible for damage beyond normal wear.

Cancellation policy

Sample terms shown for now — your final policy is confirmed before you book. Typically a 50% deposit holds your dates; cancel 30+ days out for a refund of the deposit less a small fee; inside 30 days, payments are non-refundable. Travel insurance is recommended.

FAQ

Quick answers

How many does it sleep?

Up to six — a private rear queen bedroom plus a loft with two queen beds. One full bath with a large walk-in shower.

How do I get to the beach?

A short golf-cart ride to nearly a mile of private Ocean Lakes beach with 20 marked access points. Carts aren’t included — bring your own or rent one.

Who can rent?

The primary renter must be 25 or older and present for the stay. Family-friendly home — no party groups.

Is there a gate fee?

Yes — Ocean Lakes charges a per-vehicle gate pass (~$12–$15/day) paid to the campground, separate from your rental. Two passes typically come with the stay.

What’s the minimum stay?

Peak summer is Saturday-to-Saturday; spring, fall, and winter often allow shorter 2–4 night stays.

Are pets welcome?

Please ask first — the pet policy and any fee are confirmed at booking. Service animals are always welcome.
