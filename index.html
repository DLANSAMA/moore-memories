The Resort

A whole vacation world inside the gates

Ocean Lakes is a gated, 310-acre oceanfront resort — nearly a mile of private beach, a 3-acre Sandy Harbor water park, indoor & outdoor pools, mini golf, an arcade, fishing lakes, and a general store, all a short golf-cart ride from your porch.
