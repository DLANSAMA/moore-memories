The Home

The tiny home, room by room

Welcome to Moore Memories, a bright, modern tiny home tucked into Site 1669 inside the gates of Ocean Lakes. The open-concept layout, vaulted ceilings, and walls of windows make it feel airy and wide open — with a queen bedroom plus a loft holding two more queens, it comfortably sleeps up to six.

Living & Dining

The heart of the home is a sunlit open-concept living and dining space crowned by vaulted ceilings and surrounded by windows that pull in the coastal light. There's room to gather, share a meal, and unwind after a beach day, with the sleeping loft looking down over it all.

  • Open-concept with vaulted ceilings
  • Walls of windows, bright natural light
  • Smart TV + WiFi for streaming
  • Central A/C plus ceiling fans

Kitchen

The kitchen flows right into the living and dining area, so the cook is never left out of the conversation. It comes fully equipped for everything from quick breakfasts to a full family dinner.

  • Full-size refrigerator, stove/oven, microwave
  • Coffee maker, toaster & cookware
  • Dishware, glassware & flatware
  • Open sightlines to the living space

Primary Bedroom

Set privately at the rear of the home, the primary bedroom offers a restful retreat with a comfortable queen bed — the quiet corner you'll look forward to at the end of a long beach day.

  • Queen bed at the rear of the home
  • Private, set apart from the living space
  • Fresh linens included
  • A/C and ceiling-fan comfort

Sleeping Loft

Up above the living area, the open loft makes clever use of the vaulted ceilings and holds two queen beds with a fun overlook of the space below — ideal for kids, extra guests, or anyone who loves a cozy perch.

  • Two queen beds in an open loft
  • Reached by a few steps (not a ladder)
  • Best for kids, teens & easygoing adults
  • Linens provided for all beds

Bathroom & Laundry

The full bathroom is finished with a large walk-in shower that feels surprisingly spacious for a tiny home, and a stackable washer and dryer is built right in — so you can rinse off beach towels and pack lighter.

  • Large walk-in shower
  • Stackable washer/dryer on site
  • Towels & hair dryer provided
  • Thoughtful, modern finishes

Porch, Deck & Yard

Step outside to a covered, metal-roof porch and an adjoining composite deck made for slow mornings and easy evenings. The artificial-turf yard stays green and low-maintenance, with parking for three.

  • Covered porch + composite deck
  • Low-maintenance turf yard
  • New driveway, parking for 3
  • Short golf-cart ride to the beach
