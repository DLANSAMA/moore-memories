Resort & Area

A vacation world, inside & out

Moore Memories sits at Site 1669 inside Ocean Lakes Family Campground, a gated, 310-acre oceanfront resort on the south end of Myrtle Beach. A short golf-cart ride from your covered porch puts you at nearly a mile of private beach, a 3-acre water park, indoor and outdoor pools, mini golf, an arcade, and a 12,000 sq ft general store — the freedom of a beach getaway with the safety of a self-contained, family-focused resort.
